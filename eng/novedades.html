About Gunner

Gunner is Hard Rock band from Buenos Aires, mainly influenced by 80's rock. It was formed in early 2003. They spent a long time playing all over the under music scene of Buenos Aires. Their first record, "Desire" was released in 2011 and presented during 2 years and a half around Argentina and South America. 2013 is the year when "Keep Fighting" their second album, will be out in stores. Read more

Gunner signed with Pulmonar Records for his second studio album “Keep Fighting”. The contract includes the promotion and rotation of the album all over the country and it including on PRV label group catalog as one of the newest releases of 2014. Also includes an deluxe edition only for fans wich will comments on the next months. To buy the album can write to merchandising@gunnerband.com.
